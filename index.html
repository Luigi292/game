How to Play

Game Objective

Unscramble the letters to form the correct word. Each correct answer earns you points!

Scoring System

  • 1 point per letter in the unscrambled word
  • Example: "apple" (5 letters) = 5 points
  • If you use a hint, your points will be reduced by 50%

Prize Levels

  • 100 points - Bronze Prize
  • 500 points - Silver Prize
  • 1000 points - Gold Prize & Game Completion!

Hint System

Stuck? Click the "Need Hint" button to get help, but remember - using a hint will reduce your potential points for that word by half.

Time Limit

You have 30 seconds to guess each word. The timer resets with each new word.
